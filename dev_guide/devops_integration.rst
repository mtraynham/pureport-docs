=====================================
Devops Integration
=====================================

One of the key elements of the Pureport Multicloud Fabric is the ability to
integrate it into agile development pipelines and devops methodologies.
Pureport provides integration with various frameworks, applications and
external systems to allow organizations to easily integrate Pureport's
capabilities into their systems.

Pureport provides integration with today's leading automation platforms and
continues to be active in those communities as well providing and supporting
a full suite of integrations with the Pureport Fabric API.
